Our Journey Since 1989

Over three decades of innovation, growth, and technological excellence.

89

1989 - Foundation

FRCI Ltd. was founded in Port Louis, Mauritius, with a vision to provide quality IT services to local businesses. Starting with a small team of 5 IT professionals, we focused on hardware maintenance and basic IT support.

95

1995 - Expansion

Expanded our service offerings to include network infrastructure and software solutions. Our team grew to 25 employees, and we moved to a larger office in Cyber City to accommodate our growth.

03

2003 - Microsoft Partnership

Became a Microsoft Certified Partner, marking our commitment to excellence in Microsoft technologies. This partnership allowed us to offer enhanced solutions and services to our growing client base.

10

2010 - Cloud Revolution

Embraced the cloud revolution by launching our dedicated cloud services division. We became one of the first IT companies in Mauritius to offer comprehensive cloud migration and management services.

15

2015 - Microsoft Gold Partner

Achieved Microsoft Gold Partner status, recognizing our expertise and commitment to excellence in Microsoft solutions. This milestone reflected our team's dedication to continuous learning and professional development.

20

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched our Digital Transformation practice to help businesses navigate the challenges of the pandemic. Our remote work solutions and digital collaboration tools helped many Mauritian companies maintain operations during this challenging period.

25

2025 - Present Day

Today, FRCI Ltd. stands as a leading IT solutions provider in Mauritius with over 120 IT professionals, serving 500+ clients across various industries. We continue to innovate and expand our service offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
